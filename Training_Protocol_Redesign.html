THE ONE CORRECTION THAT MATTERS MOST

Count direct isolation sets as 1.0 and synergist / indirect sets as 0.5 (Pelland 2025). Triceps from pressing, or quads from leg press, are half-sets for those muscles — so small muscles (delts, arms, calves, tibialis) need dedicated direct work to actually hit their volume. See Science → Volume·RIR.

🦵 Knee & Tendon

Built into both leg days — Mon (Lower A) & Fri (Lower B)
HOW THIS WORKS

Rehab is not a separate "3 exercises, left only" block. The evidence-based core is Heavy Slow Resistance (HSR) loading built directly into the leg-press / squat / hinge main lifts, with a supporting ring of hip, adductor, hamstring, calf, and tibialis work around the knee. Bilateral, with marginally more attention to the LEFT (the more symptomatic side).
